Transocean Offshore International Trinidad Cold Stack Nest
Project Date

July 2015 to Dec 2015

Brief Description

During preservation stage for nine ultra-deep drill ships, BTTL provided suitably qualified Offshore Labour to take the vessels from full operation through warn stack then to full Cold stack Status (laying up).

Services Engaged

Project Management and Logistics Requirements provided:

  • Qualified Manpower
  • Training for Personnel
  • Catering services
  • Technical services (electrical and Mechanical)
  • Fabrication and installation
  • Equipment Rentals
  • Offsite Fabrication
