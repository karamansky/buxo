Grown from local soil on the shores of the south coastline Buxo Trinidad and Tobago has well developed local interactions and connections. Always well known as the company that cares for the community, our Corporate and Social Responsibility is as deep rooted as our foundation.

The insights of one man in a time of rapid movement in the Energy Industry, Philip Buxo, saw the potential to promote the local service industry to the International Energy Sector, bridging the gap that would promote the local economy and move Trinidad and Tobago forward.

Dedication, determination and passion took this company from its local development to a place of forming strategic partnerships with some of the best in class International companies, whilsts keeping a sense of responsibility to the local economy. This forms the core of our operation today, or reach is global while out roots are planted locally.
